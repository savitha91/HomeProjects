These are the conclusions drawn from analysing customer Churn using telco dataset

> Average bill paid by customers is 2280Rs
> Electronic payment mode is the most used for payment
> Customer who has not availed internet service pays very minimum amount
> Preferred internet service is Fibre optic
> Discounts can be provided to Senior citizens as a part of customer retention program
> There are quiet a lot of people whose tenure is greater than 20 years
